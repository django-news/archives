### Black (22.1.0) is out of beta

<p>At long last, Black is no longer a beta product! This is the first non-beta release and the first release covered by a new stability policy. Django has committed to incorporating Black once this milestone was reached per <a href="https://cur.at/cj3cb5Q">DEP 8</a>.</p>

[https://github.com/psf/black/releases/tag/22.1.0](https://github.com/psf/black/releases/tag/22.1.0)

### Google sponsors Wagtail CMS's next-generation web content management experience

<p>When Google saw the roadmap of enhancements ahead for Wagtail, they realized how much their team will benefit and decided to provide $150,000 of corporate sponsorship to accelerate the development effort.</p>

[https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/google-sponsors-wagtail-cmss-next-generation-web-content-management-experience-301473717.html](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/google-sponsors-wagtail-cmss-next-generation-web-content-management-experience-301473717.html)
