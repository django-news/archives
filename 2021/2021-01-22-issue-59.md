## News

### Django 3.2 alpha 1 released

<p>Django 3.2 alpha 1 is now available. It represents the first stage in the 3.2 release cycle and is an opportunity for you to try out the changes coming in Django 3.2.</p>

[https://www.djangoproject.com/weblog/2021/jan/19/django-32-alpha-1-released/](https://www.djangoproject.com/weblog/2021/jan/19/django-32-alpha-1-released/)

### PyCon 2021 Call for Proposals

<p>The PyCon 2021 call for proposals is open and closes on February 12, 2021. PyCon US is a massive conference--even virtually--and in recent years the web track, specifically Django, has been underrepresented vs data science/AI/ML talks.</p>

<p>Consider applying!</p>

[https://us.pycon.org/2021/speaking/](https://us.pycon.org/2021/speaking/)
